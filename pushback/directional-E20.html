Standard Pushback Procedure

Directional Graphic for “Pushback face North”

- The aircraft (on idle thrust) shall be pushed back following the pushback until its nosewheel is at the "EOP 17" position. The aircraft shall then be towed forward following the tow line onto Taxiline A6 to face North until its nosewheel is at "EOT 18A" position.
- The aircraft may breakaway from there.

Phraseology used by Singapore Ground
“Standard pushback approved.”
